6-Day Hypertrophy Workout Split - Sri J

Day 1: Chest & Back (Push/Pull)

Chest Exercises

Exercise Sets Reps Description
Dumbbell Incline Bench Press 4 12 Targets the upper chest with a full range of motion.
Machine Chest Press 3 12 Isolates the chest with controlled pressing movement.
Cable Fly (Low to High) 3 12 Emphasizes the lower and middle chest.
Push-Ups (Weighted if possible) 3 Failure Great finisher for chest activation.

Back Exercises

Exercise Sets Reps Description
Pull-Ups 4 12 Builds upper back and lats with bodyweight resistance.
Seated Cable Row 3 12 Focuses on middle-back thickness.
Smith Machine Deadlift 3 12 Strengthens the posterior chain, focusing on back and hamstrings.
Straight Arm Lat Pull Down 3 12 Isolates the lats for a good stretch and contraction.

Day 2: Legs & Shoulders

Leg Exercises

Exercise Sets Reps Description
Leg Press 4 12 Targets the quads, hamstrings, and glutes.
Goblet Squat (with a pause) 4 12 Improves quad and glute activation while enhancing stability.
Bulgarian Split Squat 3 12 Single-leg movement focusing on quads and glutes.
Dumbbell Hip Thrust 3 12 Activates glutes with maximum tension.
Standing Calf Raise (Dropsets) 4 12 Builds strong, defined calves.

Shoulder Exercises

Exercise Sets Reps Description
Seated Dumbbell Press 4 12 Targets overall shoulder development.
Dumbbell Lateral Raise 4 12 Focuses on the side delts for width.
Rear Delt Dumbbell Fly 3 12 Isolates rear delts for balanced shoulder development.

Day 3: Arms (Biceps & Triceps)

Bicep Exercises

Exercise Sets Reps Description
Dumbbell Curl (Alternating) 4 12 Builds overall bicep mass with controlled motion.
Cable Hammer Curl (Rope) 4 12 Targets brachialis and forearm strength.
Incline Dumbbell Curl (Stretch Focus) 3 12 Emphasizes the bicep peak and stretch.
Machine Preacher Curl 3 12 Isolates the biceps with strict form.

Tricep Exercises

Exercise Sets Reps Description
Lying Dumbbell Tricep Extension 4 12 Works the long head of the triceps.
Standing Cable Overhead Extension 4 12 Stretches and contracts the triceps.
Close-Grip Bench Press (Smith Machine) 3 12 Builds tricep mass and strength.
Tricep Dip (Weighted) 3 12 Hits the lower triceps and chest as a secondary muscle.

Day 4: Chest & Back (Alternate Push/Pull)

Chest Exercises

Exercise Sets Reps Description
Flat Dumbbell Bench Press 4 12 Targets the middle chest for mass.
Machine Chest Fly (Mid-Level) 4 12 Isolates the chest with controlled stretching and contraction.
Chest Dip (Weighted) 4 12 Builds lower chest thickness and strength.
Incline Push-Ups 3 Failure A great finisher to fully fatigue the chest.

Back Exercises

Exercise Sets Reps Description
Dumbbell Row (Alternating) 4 12 Strengthens the lats and upper back with unilateral focus.
Smith Machine Row 3 12 Targets back thickness and strength.
Close-Grip Pull Down 4 12 Focuses on the lats and biceps.
T-Bar Row 3 8-12 Adds thickness to the back, especially in the middle region.

Day 5: Legs & Shoulders

Leg Exercises

Exercise Sets Reps Description
Barbell Squat (Smith Machine) 4 12 Builds overall leg strength and size.
Leg Press (Single-Leg) 3 12 Improves quad and glute focus with unilateral work.
Lying Leg Curl 3 12 Isolates hamstrings for muscle growth.
Dumbbell Rear Lunge (Deficit) 4 12 Targets the quads, hamstrings, and glutes.
Standing Calf Raise (Dropsets) 4 20 Focuses on calf hypertrophy.

Shoulder Exercises

Exercise Sets Reps Description
Cable Face Pull 4 12 Improves posture and targets rear delts.
Machine Lateral Raise (Heavy) 4 12 Builds side delt width and mass.
Dumbbell Shrug (Hold at Top) 4 12 Builds traps and upper back.

Day 6: Arms & Shrugs

Bicep Exercises

Exercise Sets Reps Description
Barbell Curl (Wide-Grip) 4 12 Builds overall bicep strength and size.
Incline Dumbbell Curl 4 12 Focuses on the stretch for maximum growth.
Spider Curl (Preacher Bench) 3 12 Isolates biceps with strict form.
Cable Hammer Curl (Rope) 4 12 Targets brachialis and forearm development.

Tricep Exercises

Exercise Sets Reps Description
Close-Grip Bench Press (Smith Machine) 4 12 Builds tricep strength and mass.
Cable Tricep Pushdown 3 12 Works the lateral head of the triceps.
Lying Dumbbell Tricep Extension 3 12 Stretches the triceps under tension.

Shrug Exercise

Exercise Sets Reps Description
Smith Machine Shrug (Hold at Top) 4 12 Develops traps and neck muscles.
